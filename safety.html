Because Your Child’s Safety Comes First

We know what it means to hand your child off to someone else — that’s why every inch of Nanny Driver is built around one thing: trust. From trained drivers to live tracking, we’ve designed every ride to give you peace of mind.

Our Safety Commitments

  • Background-Checked Drivers: Every driver passes multi-layered national, state, and local background checks. We go deeper than rideshare apps.
  • CPR & First Aid Certified: All drivers are certified in CPR and pediatric emergency care — so they’re prepared for the unexpected.
  • Behavior & Situational Training: We train our drivers in communication, child behavior, and emergency response scenarios.
  • Always Watching – Dashcams: Every vehicle is equipped with interior and exterior dashcams that are reviewed regularly for safety compliance.
  • Live Tracking: Parents can view the ride in real time, with alerts for pickups, drop-offs, and stops along the way.
  • In-App SOS Button: Both drivers and riders have access to an SOS feature that immediately alerts our safety team and notifies parents.
  • Ride Logs and Audits: Every ride is logged and monitored for quality control, allowing us to investigate and respond to any incident quickly.

Your Trust. Our Promise.

We’re not just a ride — we’re your partner in parenting. Nanny Driver is driven by one belief: if we wouldn’t put our own child in that car, we won’t put yours in it either.
