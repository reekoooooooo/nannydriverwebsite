Because Your Child’s Safety Comes First

We know what it means to hand your child off to someone else — that’s why every inch of Nanny Driver is built around one thing: trust. From trained drivers to live tracking, we’ve designed every ride to give you peace of mind.

A Parent’s Story

“I started Nanny Driver because I was tired of rushing from work, worrying if my son made it to soccer practice on time. One day, my sitter canceled last-minute, and I had no options — that feeling stuck with me. This service is built to make sure no parent has to feel that again.”

Our Safety Commitments

  • Background-Checked Drivers: Every driver passes multi-layered national, state, and local background checks. We go deeper than rideshare apps.
  • CPR & First Aid Certified: All drivers are certified in CPR and pediatric emergency care — so they’re ready for the unexpected.
  • Behavior & Situational Training: We train our drivers in communication, child behavior, and emergency response scenarios.
  • Always Watching: Dashcams in every vehicle record rides (front and rear) and are reviewed routinely for compliance and safety.
  • Live Tracking: Parents can see the car’s location and get notifications at pickup, drop-off, and during stops.
  • In-App SOS: Your child or driver can tap a panic button inside the app — instantly alerting our safety team and you.
  • Ride History & Logs: Every ride is logged and stored for safety audits. Nothing slips through the cracks.

Your Trust. Our Promise.

We’re not just a ride — we’re your partner in parenting. Nanny Driver is driven by one belief: if we wouldn’t put our own child in that car, we won’t put yours in it either.
